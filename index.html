About me

Hi, I am a Illustrator and animator currently working out of Tel Aviv, Israel.

I like to make hand made animation, handwritten type and create work by using traditional and digital media. I use watercolor and acrylic painting to make pictures and then touch them up digitally.

Besides working, i'm passionate about sea, dance, and music, which have a big impact on my animation practice as a way for exploring movement and rhythm. I believe that good storytelling packed nicely in engaging content can make miracles.
